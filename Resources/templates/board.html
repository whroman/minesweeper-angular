how to play
the game is played by revealing tiles of the grid. if a selected tile contains a mine, the player loses the game. otherwise, a digit is revealed in the tile, indicating the number of mines located in the eight adjacent tiles.
click to reveal the squares of the grid.
(your first click will never land on a mine)
shift + click or alt + click will 'flag' a tile, helping you to remember where you think a mine is hidden
