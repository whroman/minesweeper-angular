how to play
the game is played by revealing squares of the grid. if a square containing a mine is revealed, the player loses the game. otherwise, a digit is revealed in the square, indicating the amount of mines located in the eight adjacent tiles.
click to reveal the squares of the grid
your first click will never land on a mine
shift + click or alt + click will flag a tile, helping you to remember where you think a mine is hidden
